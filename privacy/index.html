Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

What do we collect?

We collect only what is necessary to deliver the product:

  • Email address — collected at checkout via Stripe
  • Stripe session ID — to link your purchase to your install token

That's it. We do not collect telemetry, usage data, agent activity, prompts, or any other behavioral data. We do not require you to create an account.

How is it collected?

Your email and payment information are collected by Stripe during checkout. Stripe processes and stores your payment card data — we never see or store card numbers, CVVs, or billing details. We receive only your email address and a session identifier from Stripe.

Why do we collect it?

  • To deliver your install token after purchase
  • To provide customer support related to your subscription
  • To manage your subscription (renewal, cancellation)

We do not use your email for marketing. The only emails you will receive are transactional: your install token and any subscription-related notices.

Who else handles your data?

We use the following service providers to operate Shoofly:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (resend.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Railway — application hosting (railway.com/legal/privacy)

These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services to us. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone else.

How long do we keep it?

Your email and Stripe session ID are retained while your subscription is active. If you cancel, we will delete your data within 30 days of cancellation upon request. Contact support@shoofly.dev to request deletion.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technology on shoofly.dev.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing support@shoofly.dev. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the lawful basis for processing your data is performance of a contract (delivering the product you purchased). For site operation, our lawful basis is legitimate interest.

Regardless of where you are located, we honor all deletion requests.

Children

Shoofly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. For active subscribers, we will notify you via the email address on file.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@shoofly.dev.

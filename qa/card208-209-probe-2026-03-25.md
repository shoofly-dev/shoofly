## Verdict

## **FAIL**

Two blocking issues remain from Card 208:

1. **shoofly-hook is not installed by install.sh** — the bundling requirement is unmet.

Card 209 hardening checks all pass cleanly.

**Recommendation:** Address F1 and F2 before marking Cards 208/209 as done. W1 and W2 should be triaged for the next patch.
